Internet Of Things

What is the Internet of Things?

The Internet of Things is defined as the interconnection of computing devices installed in everyday objects.

Why Does This Concern You?

Only one part of that definition has any relevance to you, and that is everyday objects. Internet of Things is just a fancy way of saying everything has the capability to talk to one another. Things like:

  • Lightbulbs
  • Televisions
  • Even Refrigerators
